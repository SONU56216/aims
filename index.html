About AIMS College

AIMS College is a leading educational institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. AIMS College continuously organizes seminars, workshops, tours, and technical events to ensure students gain real-life experience beyond classrooms.

College Programs & Activities

CSIT Hackathon Program

The CSIT Hackathon program at AIMS College encourages students to develop innovative solutions using technology. Students work in teams to solve real-world problems within a limited time frame, enhancing their coding, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. This program helps students gain confidence, creativity, and exposure to modern software development trends.

BHM Educational Tours

The BHM program organizes educational and industrial tours to hotels, resorts, and hospitality centers. These tours help students understand hotel operations, customer service standards, and real-life hospitality management. Practical exposure during tours plays a vital role in shaping professional skills and industry knowledge.

BBS Industrial Tour Program

The BBS Industrial Tour program allows students to visit industries, factories, and corporate offices. This program helps students understand business operations, management practices, marketing strategies, and financial systems. The tour bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world business environments.

College Programs & Events

Saraswati Puja Celebration

Saraswati Puja is celebrated every year at AIMS College with great devotion and enthusiasm. Students and teachers come together to worship Goddess Saraswati, the symbol of knowledge and wisdom. The program includes puja, cultural activities, prayers, and blessings for academic success. This event strengthens unity, discipline, and respect for education among students.

Welcome Program

The Welcome Program at AIMS College is organized to warmly receive new students. Seniors and faculty members introduce the college environment, rules, and opportunities available on campus. Cultural performances, speeches, and interactive sessions help new students feel comfortable and confident as they begin their academic journey.

Farewell Program

The Farewell Program is a memorable event organized to honor graduating students of AIMS College. Juniors express gratitude and best wishes through speeches, performances, and presentations. The program celebrates achievements, friendships, and the journey of students as they move forward toward their professional careers.
